VERY, Issue 7

 

 

 

VERY magazine is a cross disciplinary study into art and fashion, started out of New York by gallerist Uscha Pohl in the late 90s. VERY became associated with an alternative approach to fashion held by many photographers and artists at the time, featuring stories, and articles to reflect this. Photographer Angela Hill and illustrator Francois Berthoud became close collaborators of the magazine, as did many other alternative artists of the late 90s and early 2000s.
