Cine2, Issue 12, 'Mystery Train'

 

 

 

Published by Chanter on the release of the film into Japanese cinemas, this publication features interviews, articles, and imagery from Jim Jarmusch’s ‘Mystery Train’. Set in Memphis, Tennessee, the film follows three seperate stories, all rooted in their obsessions with the late Elvis Presley. With cinematography by Robbie Muller, and Jarmusch’s routine take on the weird and wonderful, this film remains one of the directors most acclaimed. The considered stylistic approach, lends itself well to print collateral, with this publication being an important reference, and incredible piece of ephemera.

All text in Japanese

Chanter / 1989 / 180 x 250 / $30
