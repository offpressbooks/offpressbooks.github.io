Figure Oh Special Issue: Takashi Murakami

 

 

 

Figurine is sealed / approximate height is 50mm

 

Figure Oh is a Japanese magazine focusing on figurines. This special issue has a feature on artist Takashi Murakami, and in particular the 'Superflat Series' he has created.

Murakami began the postmodern movement; 'superflat', in 2001, which saw a combing of centuries old Japanese art with contemporary culture. A major signifier of the movement were artists use of manga, and its formal aesthetics. Murakami's 'superflat museum' set of figurines is an accurate display of how the movement plays into commercial aspects of contemporary culture, blurring the line of high and low art.

This magazine comes with a figurine named 'Strange Melting Dob' from the 'superflat museum' series.
