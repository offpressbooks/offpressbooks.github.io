Jean Paul Gaultier, Femme Autumn/Winter 2007-2008, Lookbook

 

 

 

Jean Paul Gaultier lookbook, consisting of images from the runway. Featuring 28 looks from the 2007-2008, Autumn/Winter Femme Collection.

The lookbook is interestingly presented in landscape format, and uses a silver gelatin process for printing the images. Each look is accompanied with a list, detailing the garment, and it's product code. Although somewhat late into his career, this show is a significant look at the designers extravagance that garnered him such acclaim. A good source of inspiration and reference.

Aeffe S.p.A / 2008 / 290 x 200 / $30
