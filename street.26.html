Street, Issue 26, Vol.5

 

 

 

STREET started with the intention to introduce Japan to a euro centric style of dress, influenced mainly by Paris and London. Established in 1985, STREET was different to many other style magazines, as it only consisted of photos, with little to no text or graphic elements. The style of photography was akin to the ‘straight-up’ style adopted by I-D magazine in the early 80s. STREET magazine’s founder, and photographer, Shoichi Aoki would later go on to start widely acclaimed FRUITS magazine.

"We send fashion flavor from streets of other country by new type paper media [STREET], visually." - STREET
