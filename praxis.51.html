Praxis, Issue 51, 'Let's have a New Party!'

 

 

 

"Praxis is the internal discussion and information bulletin of the CPA." The Communist Party of Australia produced Praxis from 1970 onwards, and existed as a platform for discussion between party members. The magazine was able to be purchased by party members, and non-party members; of whom had been approved by the relevant State or District Committee upon request for purchase. Many leftist ideologies presented throughout are theories, and concerns held in todays society; offering a comparative look at Australian politics over the years.

In this issue, A special edition cover featuring Snoopy, the protagonist in Charles M. Schulz' 'Peanuts' cartoon.

CPA National Committee / August 1988 / 190 x 270 / $15
