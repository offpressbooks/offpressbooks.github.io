Crash, Issue 14, 'Fashion 360' Special Fashion Issue

 

 

 

Fashion imagery at its best, and with no mention of the usual suspects. Crash magazine has always taken an alternative approach to fashion documentation, from the photographers they work with, to the designers they feature, and this issue is a testament to this perspective. Stunning photography captures underground designers, such as Seth Shapiro (Bernadette Corporation), As Four, Ann-Sofie Back, the late Benjamin Cho, and many more from all the major fashion capitals. Two fashion stories from regular contributors Banu Cennetoglu, and Dirk Sieden Schwan are beautifully presented across double page spreads, highlighting key pieces from designers Helmut Lang, Chanel, and Prada.

In this issue Madonna - Meta Production. Prine - 4 The Love of Music. Theivery Corporation - Exotisme Hi-Fi. Bertrand Burgalat - b.b. Initials. Henri Salvador - Henri, un ami qui vous veut du bien. Roman Coppola - Family Business. Stephen Frears - Pop Fidelity.

Also Global Fashion by Frank Perrin. Modele de Mode by Oliver Saillard. London 360 by Flo Kolmer, Andreas Larsson, Angel Prunner. New York 360 by Marlene Marino, As Four. Paris 360 by Miguel Rosales. Teenage 360 by Toyin, Flo Kolmer. Custom 360 by Jean-Francois Carly. Art, Design, Fashion 360 by Christopher Sturman, Morgan Legall, Banu Cennetoglu, Dirk Seiden.

Crash Production / October-November 2000 / 210 x 275 / $60
