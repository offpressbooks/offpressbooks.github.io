Pattern's by Pineapple Studios, Volume 1

 

 

 

 

'Pattern's' is a unique output of Japanese design studio Pineapple Studios. Each volume of 'Patterns' focuses on a symbol, or theme, in which artist Hironori Yasuda has translated into a series of patterns. All but the first and last page of each volume contain a unique pattern.

Yasuda remains somewhat of a mystery in the field of graphic design, with Pineapple Studios, and in particular these books, being the only source of information on the artist. 'Pattern's' was intended to be used as an open source library for industry professionals; allowing them to use Yasuda's artwork for creative and/or commercial purposes. The books have now become a rare piece of design history, and remain a source of inspiration for designers, globally.

Volume 1 of 'Pattern's' focuses on 'Dot'.

K.D.C Co / 1986 / First Edition / 200 x 290 / $100
