Go-Sees Juergen Teller

 

 

 

Noted as one of fashions most renowned photographers, Juergen Teller's Go-Sees is a documentation of models that visited his studio throughout 1998 to 1999. Teller's response to the over-glamorisation, and high production of fashion photography in the 1980s is seen here, in arguably its truest form. With over 400 models visiting his West London studio, Go-Sees gives a behind the scenes look into the faces of 90s fashion, whilst also capturing individual styles not represented in mainstream media of the time. These photographs have become some of Teller's most notable works, and a source of inspiration for any fashion aficionado.

Scalo Verlag AG / 1999 / First Edition / Hardcover / 170 x 240 / $260
