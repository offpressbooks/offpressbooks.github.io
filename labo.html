Labo, Vol.031224, Photographic Magazine

 

 

 

Photographic magazine featuring the work of Japanese photographers Jun Hamanaka, Yasuo Katayama, Tsuyoshi Numata, Nagaru Funamizu, and Toshiyuki Ito.

Each artist presents a series of photographs, documenting various rural landscapes; giving a glimpse into the peacefulness of life on the outer skirts of society. With little information on the artists, and the publication itself, this becomes a beautifully puzzling piece of photographic works.

Nagaru Funamizu / 2003 / 220 x 290 / $20
