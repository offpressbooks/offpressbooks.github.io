Comme Des Garcons SHIRT, Spring/Summer 2006, Press Lookbook

 

 

 

Comme Des Garcons SHIRT Press Lookbook from the 2006 Spring/Summer collection, featuring over 35 looks, of spiral bound photographic plates.

This spiral bound booklet was most likely published in house by Comme Des Garcons. The photographs are bound directly after being printed, with the underside of the image reading; 'FUJIFILM FUJICOLOR Ever-Beauty Paper for LASER'. This publication offers a varied perspective on the pieces in the show, and the show itself.

unknown / 2006 / 110 x 150 / $80 / some pages have slight tears
