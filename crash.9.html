Crash, Issue 9, 'Invaders 2000'

 

 

 

This issue includes a special feature on French industrial designer Ito Morabito; focusing on his purist vision for contemporary design. It wouldn't be Crash without stunning imagery, with two fashion stories to note, shot by Sinisha, and Jonathan Frantini. Also in this issue; a comparative piece on mannequins and models, and the role contemporary photography plays in capturing garments.

In this issue Reservoirs - Vintage attitude, Fingerboard, Pokemon mania, Meier, Kitano, Label Gigolo, Shinju Gumi, Demon, Jackson, Elegia, Letfield, Hello Kitty, Boo.com. L’effet blairwitch. Ora ito - baad goods. Kubrik - le projet inedit. Gopher - future groove. Everything but respect. The dark side of oizo. Dumont - l’humanite de chair. Serie 1 - radio head x 2.Generation Clip - Bardou, Jacquet, Gondry, Poiraud, Caudron. Defiles d’attitude - Raf Simons, Helmut Lang, Martin Margiela, BLESS, Hussein Chalayan. De Mannequins Comme Attitudes. Serie 2 - YSL 2000. Serie 3 - 9/9/99.Xavier Veilhan - la foret.
